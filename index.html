MOONSHOT

REAL PHYSICS · REAL ROCKETS · REAL MOON

Launch from Earth. Slingshot to the Moon. Get home alive.

Newton gives you gravity from Earth and Moon. Atmosphere fights you on the way up and wants to kill you on the way down. Use your stages. Time warp in vacuum. Flip the capsule around so the heat shield takes the heat.
